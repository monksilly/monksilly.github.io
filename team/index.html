// About MonkSilly

WHO WE ARE

MonkSilly is a small team of developers doing silly things inside White Knuckle's SUB-STRUCTURE 17. We make mods — some useful, some chaotic, one still held together with duct tape and determination.

We're based on GitHub and Thunderstore. Our mods are free and open-source. If something explodes, it was probably intentional. If it wasn't, galfar.exe is already fixing it.

Want to get involved, report a bug, or just hang out? Join us on Discord — the link is below and also in the nav and the footer and on every other page because we really want you to join.

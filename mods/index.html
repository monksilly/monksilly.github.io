THE MODS

WKLib
v1.0.0 STABLE LIBRARY

The backbone of everything we build. WKLib is a shared utility library that provides common hooks, APIs, and infrastructure for all MonkSilly mods — and any other mod that wants to play nicely with our ecosystem. If you're building on White Knuckle, start here.

Custom Gamemodes
v1.0.0 STABLE

Tired of playing White Knuckle the normal way? Same. Custom Gamemodes lets you and your friends flip the rules with modifiers — make SUB-STRUCTURE 17 harder, weirder, or straight-up unhinged. The shaft doesn't care. You do.

ResourcefulHands
v1.0.0 STABLE

Your hands are your only tools in White Knuckle — so let's make them actually useful. ResourcefulHands expands the interaction system and gives modders a public API to register custom hand behaviors. Hold on tight. Or don't. You won't.

WKMultiplayer
IN DEV RC-001

The big one. WKMultiplayer brings real co-op to White Knuckle — climb with a friend, fall together. Built on Steam P2P with a server-authoritative tick loop. This is the most ambitious and genuinely unhinged thing we've tried. RC builds are on GitHub Releases — grab them early, break things, tell us what exploded.

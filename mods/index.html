THE MODS

WKLib
v0.0.4 STABLE LIBRARY

The backbone of everything we build. WKLib is a shared utility library that provides common hooks, APIs, and infrastructure for all MonkSilly mods — and any other mod that wants to play nicely with other mods. If you're making mods for White Knuckle, start here.

Custom Gamemodes
v0.1.4 STABLE

Tired of playing White Knuckle the normal way? Same. Custom Gamemodes lets you play custom made gamemodes — play SUB-STRUCTURE 42 or repair a generator. The substructure doesn't care. You do.

ResourcefulHands
v0.10.0 WIP

Your hands are your only precious thing in White Knuckle — so let's make them actually pretty. ResourcefulHands expands the cosmetics system and gives modders an ability to make custom hand cosmetics. Hold on tight. Or don't. You won't.

WKMultiplayer
IN DEV RC-???

The big one. WKMultiplayer brings real co-op to White Knuckle — climb with a friend, fall together. Built on Steam P2P with a server-authoritative tick loop. This is the most ambitious and genuinely unhinged thing we've tried. RC builds will be on GitHub Releases — grab them early, break things, tell us what exploded.
